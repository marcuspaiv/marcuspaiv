## 🚀 About Me

I'm a geologist focused on **Petroleum Geology**, currently developing my Master's dissertation at UNICAMP on **structural modeling of Pre-Salt reservoirs in the Santos Basin**, in partnership with Shell. I combine a solid background in geosciences with a growing interest in **Python, data analysis, and artificial intelligence** applied to geology.

I have experience in mining, geophysics, geotechnics, renewable energy, and geoprocessing — as well as international experience in Ireland and Norway, which shaped my adaptability, teamwork, and fluent English communication skills.

- 🔹 Research in **Pre-Salt reservoir modeling** (Santos Basin)
- 🔹 Experience with **Python for geological data analysis**
- 🔹 Growing interest in **AI and Machine Learning applied to Geosciences**
- 🔹 Background in **wind/solar energy**, **mining**, and **geotechnics**
- 🔹 International experience: Ireland 🇮🇪 and Norway 🇳🇴
